```

```

Exa returns permanent, clickable URLs:
- Rolling Stone: https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/the-making-of-radioheads-in-rainbows-20120427

### Content: Training Data vs Real Article Text

**Current:** Gemini generates facts from training data. No article text in the prompt.

**Exa:** Gemini receives actual article highlights. For example, the Soundfly harmonic analysis includes:
- Ed O'Brien's specific arpeggio pattern and shell voicings
- Colin Greenwood's bass economy ("master of economy")
- How layered arpeggios create momentum without adding new material
- Comparison with Lianne La Havas's cover arrangement

This makes nerd-tier nuggets significantly richer and more accurate.

### Quotes: Generated vs Real

**Current:** Gemini writes quotes from memory -- may fabricate or misattribute.

**Exa:** Gemini can cite actual passages from the highlights, e.g.:
> "The focus on layering complementary rhythms to enhance the form of a song adds quite a vast array of possibilities."
